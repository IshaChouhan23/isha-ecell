ABOUT US !!

An E-cell, short for Entrepreneurship Cell, is a student-run organization in educational institutions focused on fostering entrepreneurship. It organizes workshops, competitions, guest lectures, and networking events to help students develop entrepreneurial skills, connect with industry experts, and launch their own startups.
